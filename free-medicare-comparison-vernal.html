Free Medicare Plan Comparison — Vernal & Uintah Basin

Comparing Medicare plans should not cost you anything. Vernal Medicare provides free, side-by-side plan comparisons for every Medicare beneficiary in the Uintah Basin. Use our online comparison tool or call for a personalized review with a licensed agent.

Compare Plans Online

Our Medicare plan comparison tool lets you enter your zip code, doctors, prescriptions, and preferred pharmacy to see available plans ranked by estimated annual cost. The tool is powered by Sunfire Matrix — the same platform used by licensed agents nationwide.

Launch Plan Comparison Tool

What the Comparison Includes

Medicare Advantage plans (HMO and PPO) with estimated annual costs, Medicare Supplement (Medigap) quotes for Plan G and Plan N, Part D prescription drug plan comparisons based on your medications, pharmacy cost differences (Smith's, Walgreens, mail-order), doctor and hospital network verification, and extra benefit comparisons (dental, vision, hearing, fitness).

Personalized Help Available
