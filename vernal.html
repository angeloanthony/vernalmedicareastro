Enrollment windows (quick guide)

  • AEP: Oct 15–Dec 7 (changes start Jan 1).
  • OEP (MA): Jan 1–Mar 31 (one change if you’re on an MA plan).
  • IEP/ICEP: around your 65th birthday or first eligibility.
  • SEPs: moving, losing coverage, Extra Help/Medicaid, and more.
