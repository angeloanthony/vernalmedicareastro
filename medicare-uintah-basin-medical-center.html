Medicare Plans for Uintah Basin Medical Center — Roosevelt, Utah

Uintah Basin Medical Center (formerly Uintah Basin Healthcare) in Roosevelt is the primary hospital for western Duchesne County. For Roosevelt, Duchesne, and surrounding residents, ensuring your Medicare plan covers this facility is essential.

Original Medicare Coverage

Uintah Basin Medical Center accepts Original Medicare. Part A covers inpatient stays with standard deductibles and coinsurance. Part B covers outpatient services, emergency visits, and physician services. Medigap policies work here because Medigap is accepted anywhere Original Medicare is accepted.

Medicare Advantage Coverage

If you have a Medicare Advantage plan, verify that Uintah Basin Medical Center is in your plan's network. Network participation can change year to year. We verify hospital coverage as part of every plan comparison for Roosevelt and Duchesne County residents.

Services at Uintah Basin Medical Center

The facility offers emergency services, inpatient care, surgery, obstetrics, radiology and imaging, laboratory, rehabilitation, and specialty clinics. Medicare covers medically necessary services at standard rates when the facility is in-network or when using Original Medicare.
